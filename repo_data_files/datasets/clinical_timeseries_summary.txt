Dataset Name: ICUVitals2025
Samples: 10,500 patient admissions
Features:
- HR (heart rate)
- BP (blood pressure)
- SpO2 (oxygen saturation)
Time Granularity: 1-min intervals
Label: Mortality (binary)
Notes:
- De-identified in compliance with HIPAA
- Data bias: Overrepresentation of patients aged 60+
